Ashurah Day

Ashurah is the 10th day of Muharram, the first month of the Islamic calendar. It is a significant day in Islamic history, particularly for all Muslims, who commemorate the martyrdom of Imam Hussein (peace be upon him) at the Battle of Karbala in 680 CE.
